ABOUT US

Society of Renewable Energy Institut Teknologi Sepuluh Nopember (SRE-ITS) exists as a student organization dedicated to increasing student interest and knowledge of new and renewable energy. SRE ITS exists to encourage students at ITS and throughout Indonesia to be concerned about the renewable energy sector

Missions

Our Missions


Creating a professional scope of work based on the principle of togetherness.

Increase the insight of management and members in the field of renewable energy.

Accommodate the self-development of management and members in the field of renewable energy.

Facilitate management and members regarding the needs in the field of renewable energy

Departements


Public Relations
